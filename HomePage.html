The College of Engineering and Petroleum hosts the 41st Engineering Design Exhibition

he Director of the Engineering Training and Alumni Center Dr. Duaij Al-Rukaiby said that the Engineering Design Exhibition is a success story of several generations of over 12000 students who graduated under the supervision of over 190 faculty members.
